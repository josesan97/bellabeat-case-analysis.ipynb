# Bellabeat Case Study: Understanding User Behavior Through Smart Device Data
Google Analytics Bellabeat Study Case

## Project Overview
This case study analyzes smart device fitness data to identify trends in physical activity, sleep habits, and user behavior. The objective is to generate actionable insights that can help Bellabeat improve its marketing strategy, enhance user engagement, and support data-driven business decisions.

## Business Task
Analyze smart device usage data to discover consumer trends and provide recommendations that can support Bellabeat's marketing strategy and future business decisions.

## Dataset
The dataset used in this project comes from Fitbit Fitness Tracker Data and contains information related to physical activity, sleep monitoring, and calorie expenditure. The data was used to explore user habits and identify trends associated with health and wellness.

## Key Findings
- A considerable portion of users maintain sedentary lifestyles.
- Sunday recorded the lowest average number of daily steps.
- Sunday also showed the highest average sleep duration.
- A strong positive correlation (0.61) was found between VeryActiveMinutes and Calories burned.
- A moderate positive correlation (0.59) was found between TotalSteps and Calories burned.
- Higher levels of physical activity are generally associated with greater calorie expenditure.

## Recommendations
Based on the analysis, the following recommendations are proposed:

- Implement personalized notifications and reminders that encourage users to remain active and avoid long periods of inactivity.
- Introduce reminders that promote healthy sleep schedules and better recovery habits.
- Develop daily and weekly activity goals related to steps, calories burned, and active minutes.
- Create challenges, rewards, or gamification features to increase user engagement and motivation.
- Provide daily and weekly health summaries displaying steps, calories burned, sleep hours, and activity levels.
- Encourage healthier weekend routines, particularly on Sundays, when users appear to be less physically active.
- Highlight activity tracking, calorie expenditure, and sleep monitoring features in future marketing campaigns and product improvements.

## Conclusion
The analysis provided valuable insights into user activity patterns, sleep habits, and overall wellness behavior. The findings suggest that while many users maintain active lifestyles, a significant number remain sedentary. By implementing targeted engagement strategies, activity reminders, and personalized health insights, Bellabeat can enhance user experience, encourage healthier habits, and support future business growth through data-driven decision-making.
